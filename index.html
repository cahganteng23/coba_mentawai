Paradise Found: Katiet, Sipora Island

Located in the heart of surfing paradise, Sipora Island in the Mentawai archipelago is home to some of the world's most perfect waves. Our surfboards are crafted by local shapers who understand the unique conditions of these legendary breaks.

Katiet (also known as Lance's Right) is one of the most famous waves in the Mentawais - a picture-perfect right-hand reef break that delivers incredible barrels and wall sections when conditions align.
